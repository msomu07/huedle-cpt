INSTRUCTIONS

home play

✧ The objective of this game is for you to guess the RGB value of the randomly generated color(s).

✧ In level 1, you will get six chances to guess one color

✧ In level 2, you will get ten chances to guess three colors simultaneously

✧ You will know how close your RGB values are to the actual answer by the coloring of the input values after they are entered. This diagram allows you to determine how far your guess is from the actual answer. You can always refer to it on the game page by clicking the "instructions" button at the top.

✧ When an RGB input that you guessed is within 5 of the actual value, that square will turn green

✧ When all the input squares are green, you've won the game!
